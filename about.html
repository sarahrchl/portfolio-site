About

Sarah Delluta is a Manila-based Photographer that specializes in product & concert photography.

She is a product of Media Production from Assumption College, San Lorenzo.

In 2014, she started as a Concert Photographer covering live music for different publishing platforms like Manila Concert Scene, AsiaLive365, and Manila Korean Times.

Since pandemic happened, concerts got cancelled, so she decided to focus on helping start-up entrepreneurs in providing their photography and marketing needs.
